RECIPE FOR THE BEST CINNAMON ROLLS

Ingridients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • Two 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cuo granulated sugar
  • 1 egg+1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • one 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with yeast,sugar and eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into a large dough and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for two hours
  6. After the dough has doubled in size,roll it out into a large rectangle
